Why Post-Operative Lymphatic Drainage Is Essential After Surgery

After surgery, the lymphatic system becomes overwhelmed. Swelling, pressure, tightness, and fluid buildup slow the body’s ability to heal efficiently. Post-operative lymphatic drainage is a gentle, specialized technique that supports fluid movement and reduces internal pressure during recovery.
